About Us

Fine Food Finder is an innovative food finding application that allows you to discover a vast range of restaurants. It is available for any user and is completely free to use.

Usage Guide

Searching for restaurants

  1. Click on the search bar on the home page.
  2. Type in a suburb or street name you wish to search for restaurants in.
  3. Click on a restaurant to view its page. This page contains the restaurant's menu, rating, address, contact details and other general information.

Creating an account

  1. Click on 'Sign Up' at the top right of any page.
  2. Enter in a unique username, password of at least 8 characters and a valid email address.
  3. Click 'Create Account'. You will be redirected to the login page where you can then enter your new username and password to log in.

Sorting restaurants

After searching for an address, you will be taken to a results page displaying a list of restaurants. There are two buttons above this list that can be used to sort it:

  • Clicking 'Rating' will sort the list based on restaurant ratings.
  • Clicking 'Avg. Price' will sort the list based on average prices.

Sorting restaurant menus

After loading a restaurant's page, a menu for that particular restaurant is shown. There are two buttons above the menu that can be used to sort it. Clicking '$' will sort items in ascending order based on their price. Clicking it a second time will sort them in descending order. Clicking    will reset the menu back to its default ordering.
